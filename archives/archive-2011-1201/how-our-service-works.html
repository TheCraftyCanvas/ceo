How Our Service Works

We know about the periodic frustration, fear, despair, confusion, sadness and sorrow experienced by every parent of a child with special needs. And we know about the anxiety inherent to every search for more traditional schools and colleges.

At Creative Educational Options, we offer you more than a list of places! We offer a sympathetic ear--and emotional support--every step of the way.

WE ARE YOUR ADVOCATE AND YOUR CASE MANAGER

Because of the consistency of communication among all parties invested in your child's care and education, we can help you to plan a smooth and timely transition either to home or to the next appropriate placement. Be it a public school, less restrictive private school, college, or unconventional post-secondary venue, you can count on us for the help and support that you and your child deserve through every step of this challenging process.
