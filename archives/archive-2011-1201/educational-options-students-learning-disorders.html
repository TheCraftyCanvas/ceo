Educational Alternatives for Students with Learning Disorders, Learning Disabilities, and Pervasive Developmental Disorders

Parents of children with any learning disorders, learning disabilities (LD), or pervasive developmental disorders (PDD) know that finding effective academic and personal support for conditions such as ADD, ADHD, Dyslexia, Autism, Aspbergers' Syndrome is critical to a positive educational experience for each and every student.
