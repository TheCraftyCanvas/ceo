What should you expect to pay for educational consulting services?

We understand that our fee represents an additional expense for you, and it might be difficult to justify, given the significant cost of special schools and treatment programs.

Don't be penny-wise and pound-foolish

In most cases, our fee is less than the cost of one month at a boarding school or treatment facility. It's foolish to bypass the service of an independent educational consultant, and heed the advice of a marketer paid by a program to reel you in.

We accept no fees or commissions from any of the programs to which we make referrals. Our recommendations are unbiased, and our loyalty is solely to our clients!
