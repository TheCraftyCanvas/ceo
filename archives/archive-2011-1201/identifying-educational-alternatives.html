Helping to Identify Educational Alternatives for Struggling Students

If your child or teenager is struggling at school for any number of reasons, chances are the stress and frustration of steering them in the right direction is causing unwanted arguments and difficulties at home. Finding the right balance between school and individual support is critical to seeing a student succeed.

Types of Educational Options Available

Because of the assortment of issues and challenges kids, teenagers, and young adults encounter, they may be limited in their ability to succeed at school. At Creative Educational Options, we can help identify and narrow down choices for:

  • Preschool programs for little ones with and without special needs
  • Independent day schools for average, gifted and learning disabled students
  • Traditional, emotional growth and LD boarding schools
  • Vocational Schools & Technical Schools
  • Private Schools
  • Alternative schooling for children with behavioral issues
  • Special schools for students with ...
  • Therapeutic Boarding Schools
  • Colleges and Universities with LD Support
  • Nontraditional Colleges
  • Preparatory Schools
  • Post-Secondary Programs
