From points west along the Pennsylvania Turnpike:

  1. Exit at Valley Forge.
  2. Go straight on I-76 East for 3 miles and take Exit 330/Gulph Mills/Route 320.
  3. Merge onto Route 320 South (which becomes Montgomery Avenue) and travel approximately 3 miles.
  4. Turn right onto Airdale Road and travel .3 mile.
  5. Cross Lancaster Avenue/US-30 and enter the parking lot for Rosemont Plaza Apartments, 1062 E. Lancaster Avenue.
  6. Park in any legal spot and enter through the front door.
  7. Upon entering, walk across the lobby and into the first corridor. Suite 16 is on your left.

From Valley Forge/King of Prussia:

  1. Take South Gulph Road for approximately 5.7 miles. (South Gulph will become Montgomery Avenue midway through.)
  2. At Airdale Road, turn right and travel .3 mile.
  3. Cross Lancaster Avenue/U-S 30 and enter the parking lot for Rosemont Plaza Apartments, 1062 E. Lancaster Avenue.

Follow red italicized directions above (steps 6 and 7 from points west along the PA Turnpike).

From Interstate 476:

  1. Take Exit 13/St. Davids/Villanova and bear right onto Lancaster Avenue/US-30 towards Villanova.
  2. Travel 2.2 miles and turn right into Rosemont Plaza Apartments, 1062 E. Lancaster Avenue.
  3. Park in any legal spot and enter through the front door.
  4. Upon entering, walk across the lobby and into the first corridor. Suite 16 is on your left.

From the Allentown area:

  1. Take US-22 West for 4.1 miles to PA Turnpike/I-476 South.
  2. Follow I-476 South for 45 miles to Exit 13.

Follow directions above entitled "From Interstate 476".

From the Princeton, NJ area:

  1. Take US-206 South to I-95 South.
  2. Go 12.6 miles to Exit 46-B/US-1 South towards Langhorne.
  3. Drive 6.5 miles to the PA Turnpike exit and take I-276 West Towards Harrisburg.
  4. Travel 17.6 miles to Exit 20 (Mid-County Exit) / Northeast Extension / Allentown / Philadelphia / Chester. You MUST stay in the LEFTHAND lane as you exit.
  5. Follow I-476 South for 7.4 miles to Exit 13.

Follow directions above entitled "From Interstate 476".

From Central Philadelphia:

  1. Take I-76 West towards Valley Forge.
  2. Drive 13 miles to Exit 331-A and merge left onto I-476 South towards Chester.
  3. Follow I-476 South for 2.6 miles to Exit 13.

Follow directions above entitled "From Interstate 476".

From Southern New Jersey:

  1. Take the Ben Franklin Bridge or Walt Whitman Bridge across to Philadelphia and follow signs to I-76 West towards Valley Forge.
  2. Travel 13 miles to Exit 331-A and merge left onto I-476 South towards Chester.
  3. Follow I-476 South for 2.6 miles to Exit 13.

Follow directions above entitled "From Interstate 476".

From Wilmington, DE and Chester, PA:

  1. Take I-95 North towards Chester/Philadelphia for 14 miles.
  2. Take Exit 7/I-476 North towards Plymouth Meeting for 13.5 miles to exit 13.

Follow directions above entitled "From Interstate 476".
