We Can Help!

Creative Educational Options is a therapeutic and educational consultation service—operated by a licensed psychologist—for kids and young adults with a broad range of needs, skills, interests and goals. We identify schools, camps, treatment programs and post-secondary options for clients with academic, social, emotional and behavioral challenges.

And we find boarding schools, summer programs, gap year adventures and colleges for average and gifted students.

In the special-needs domain, our mission is to halt the cycle of failure, and introduce new patterns of happiness and success, for struggling clients, ages 3 to 63, with one or more of the following challenges:

  • Attention Deficit (Hyperactivity) Disorder
  • Autism Spectrum Disorders, including Asperger’s Disorder
  • Language-Based and Nonverbal Learning Disabilities
  • School Avoidance
  • Social Struggles and Bullying
  • Chronic Medical Conditions
  • Depression and Bipolar Disorder
  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Borderline and Other Personality Disorders
  • Trauma and Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder
  • Adoption and Attachment Issues
  • Suicidal Thoughts and Attempts
  • Oppositional Defiant Disorder
  • Aggression
  • Running Away
  • Substance Abuse
  • Computer and Gaming Addictions
  • Sexual Abuse, Promiscuity and Perpetration
  • Eating Disorders
  • Cutting
  • Legal problems
