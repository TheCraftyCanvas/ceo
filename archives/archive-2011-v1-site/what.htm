Every client is different, so we’re familiar with lots of different kinds of options. In eastern Pennsylvania, our knowledge base includes:
* Preschool programs for little ones with and without special needs
* Independent day schools for average, gifted and learning disabled students
* Inpatient and outpatient mental health care for children, teens and adults
* Substance abuse treatment for clients of all ages
* Competent neurologists, psychiatrists, psychologists, social workers, allied therapists and testers

Casting a wider net, we're also familiar with scores of services throughout - and outside of - the United States. We match clients with:
* Traditional, emotional growth and LD boarding schools
* Therapeutic wilderness and ranch programs
* Residential treatment centers
* Psychiatric hospitals
* Post-secondary vocational, life skills and college preparatory programs for young adults with special learning or emotional needs
* "Gap year" programs for average and gifted kids who want extra time before heading to college

For parents considering therapeutic placements, we can:
* Assist in seeking financial support from public school districts and insurance companies
* Make referrals to attorneys specializing in education law
* Recommend professionals who can transport children to school and treatment facilities
