Creative Educational Options, LLC is a comprehensive educational consultation and referral service—operated by a licensed psychologist—for kids and young adults with a broad range of needs, skills, interests and goals. We identify schools, camps, treatment programs and post-secondary options for clients with developmental, academic, social, emotional and behavioral challenges.

And we find boarding schools, summer programs, “gap year” adventures and colleges for average and gifted students.

In the special-needs domain, our mission is to halt the cycle of failure, and introduce new patterns of happiness and success, for struggling clients from 3 to 33 with one or more of the following challenges:

* Attention Deficit Disorder
* Autism spectrum disorders
* Learning disabilities
* Neurological impairments
* Chronic medical conditions
* Depression/Bipolar Disorder
* Anxiety disorders
* School phobia
* Oppositional Defiant Disorder
* Adoption issues
* Reactive Attachment Disorder
* Aggression
* Runaway history
* Substance abuse
* Sexual abuse, promiscuity or perpetration
* Eating disorders
* Legal problems
