Creative Educational Options, LLC is many things. We are a crisis intervention service for kids on the wrong path. We offer hope to youngsters who know far too much about academic frustration, social failure, emotional pain, misguided behaviors.

We’re the service of choice for parents who are losing hope…who see their kids going down the tubes and their dreams for the future evaporating.

We’re the folks who can turn lives around…by matching struggling children, teens and young adults with the best schools and treatment programs available.

Creative Educational Options, LLC is your LAST, BEST SHOT at helping your special-needs child.
