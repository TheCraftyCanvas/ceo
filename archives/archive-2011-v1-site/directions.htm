  From the PA Turnpike going East:
    Exit at Valley Forge.
    Go straight on I-76 East for 3 miles and take Exit 330/Gulph Mills/Route 320.
    Merge onto Route 320 South (which becomes Montgomery Avenue) and travel approximately 3 miles.
    Turn right onto Airdale Road and travel .3 mile.
    Cross Lancaster Avenue/US-30 and enter the parking lot for Rosemont Plaza Apartments, 1062 E. Lancaster Avenue.
Park in any legal spot and enter through the front door.
Walk across the lobby and into the first corridor.
Suite 16 is on your left.

If you have questions during the trip, call 610-526-9300.

    Cross Lancaster Avenue/U-S 30 and enter the parking lot for Rosemont Plaza Apartments, 1062 E. Lancaster Avenue.
