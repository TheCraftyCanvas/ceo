What We Do

Every client is different, so we’re familiar with lots of different kinds of options. In eastern Pennsylvania, northern Delaware, and south/central New Jersey, our knowledge base includes:

  • Independent day schools for average, gifted and learning disabled students
  • Inpatient and outpatient mental health care for struggling children, teens and adults
  • Addiction and eating disorders treatment for clients of all ages
  • Competent local neurologists, developmental pediatricians, psychiatrists, social workers, allied therapists and psycho-educational testers

Casting a wider net, we’re also knowledgeable about scores of services throughout—and outside of—the United States. We match clients with:

  • Conventional boarding schools for average and gifted students
  • Boarding schools for students with learning disabilities and social deficits
  • Therapeutic wilderness, farming and sailing programs
  • Multiple levels of mental health care
  • Treatment for a wide range of addictions, including drugs, alcohol, gambling, sex, social networking and video games
  • Treatment for eating disorders, cutting, and other forms of self-injury
  • Post-secondary vocational, life skills and college preparatory programs for young adults with ADHD, learning challenges or emotional needs
  • "Gap year" programs for average and gifted kids who want extra time before heading to college
